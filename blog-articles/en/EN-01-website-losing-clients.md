## The first impression problem

You have approximately 5 seconds to convince a visitor to stay. In that window they form a complete impression of your business — your professionalism, your credibility, your relevance to their problem.

If your website loads slowly, looks dated or fails to communicate what you do and who you serve within those first seconds — they leave. They go to your competitor. And they never come back.

## The five problems we see on almost every site we audit

**Problem 1: Slow loading speeds**

Google made it official in 2021: page speed is a ranking factor. Sites scoring below 70 on PageSpeed Insights are penalised in search results — meaning fewer people find you in the first place.

53% of mobile visitors abandon a page taking more than 3 seconds to load. Most common causes: unoptimised images, cheap shared hosting, too many plugins, no CDN.

**Problem 2: A value proposition that doesn't land**

Your homepage headline must answer three questions instantly:
1. What do you do?  2. Who do you do it for?  3. Why should I care?

❌ "Welcome to our website" / "Innovative solutions for modern challenges"
✅ "We build websites for professional services firms that need to look sharper and win better clients"

**Problem 3: No visible trust signals**

No testimonials, no client logos, no results — you're asking visitors to trust you on faith. Most won't. Put your strongest trust signal within the first screenful of the homepage.

**Problem 4: Contact process with too much friction**

Is your CTA visible without scrolling? Does it use action language? Is your phone number on every page? Every extra click or scroll costs you enquiries.

❌ "Contact us" / "Learn more"
✅ "Get a free quote" / "Start your project today"

**Problem 5: Not ranking on Google**

Missing title tags, no schema.org markup, no sitemap in Search Console, no backlinks — these aren't advanced tactics. They're basic hygiene. Not having them is the equivalent of a great shop with no sign above the door.

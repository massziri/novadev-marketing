In between sits a large segment of SMEs, consultants and professional services firms that need a genuinely premium online presence — but have been poorly served by both ends of the market.

"A business website isn't a brochure — it's your most active sales tool," said the Nova Dev founding team. "We built our process around delivering real commercial outcomes: faster load times, stronger SEO, clearer messaging and better conversion rates. At a price point that makes sense for growing businesses."
